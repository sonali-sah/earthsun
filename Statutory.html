Our Statutory Compliance Services Include

PF & ESI Compliance

Ensure timely registration, deductions, filings, and returns for Provident Fund and Employee State Insurance.

Labor Law Audits

Periodic internal audits to verify adherence to all applicable labor regulations and standards.

Statutory Registrations

Facilitate mandatory registrations under various labor laws including Shops & Establishments, CLRA, and more.

Timely Filing & Returns

Accurate and on-time filing of monthly, quarterly, and annual statutory returns to avoid penalties.

Contract Labor Compliance

Manage all statutory requirements related to contract labor engagement, licensing, and documentation.

Factory Act & Establishment Compliance

Assistance with compliance under Factories Act, Shops & Establishments Act, and other relevant laws.

Gratuity & Bonus Act Compliance

Support with eligibility checks, calculations, and payments under Gratuity and Bonus Acts.

Record Maintenance & Registers

Maintain statutory registers and records in formats as prescribed under applicable laws.
