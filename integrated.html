Comprehensive Facility Solutions Tailored for Every Need

At EarthSun Life Development Pvt Ltd, we understand that managing the day-to-day operations of a facility is vital for business success, yet it can often become overwhelming and distract from core business functions. That’s why we offer Integrated Facility Management Services, designed to ensure your facility runs smoothly, efficiently, and sustainably. From maintenance and housekeeping to security and energy management, our comprehensive services help create a productive and safe environment for your employees and visitors.

Our Integrated Facility Management Services Include

Housekeeping Services

Professional cleaning and sanitization to maintain hygiene and aesthetics across your facility.

Security Management

Trained personnel and smart surveillance solutions to ensure 24/7 facility protection.

Energy Management

Optimize energy usage through automation, monitoring, and sustainability solutions.

Technical Maintenance

Preventive and corrective maintenance of electrical, HVAC, and plumbing systems.

Landscaping

Enhancing green spaces through regular gardening, pruning, and outdoor maintenance.

Statutory Compliance

Ensure adherence to labor laws, fire safety, and regulatory frameworks with expert support.

Pest Control

Comprehensive pest management to prevent infestations and maintain a hygienic environment.

Waste Management

Eco-friendly waste collection, segregation, and disposal aligned with sustainability goals.

EarthSun Life offers expert-led training and personalized placement services to help you succeed in the tech industry. Empowering talent with the skills needed for a bright future.
